About WorkRightsUK

WorkRightsUK is designed as a static-first project focused on making core UK employment rights easier to understand through simple tools, guides, and source-backed explanations.

What this project is

  • A small, trusted product-style site
  • Calculator-first rather than article-first
  • Built to stay cheap and simple on static hosting
  • Ready to expand into related rights topics later
  • Includes a growing sources area for transparency
  • Includes a contact and trust page for clearer signposting

How we check pages

Reviewed dates and source links shown clearly

Key pages are designed to show reviewed dates, official source links, scope limits and next-step routes so users can see what the page covers and when to recheck it.

What this project is not

  • Not legal advice
  • Not a law firm site
  • Not a generic employment-law content site
  • Still improving over time as pages are checked and updated

© 2026 WorkRightsUK

General information only. It is not legal, tax, payroll or personalised HR advice.

Before relying on a figure or explanation: check the official source links on the page and get specialist advice if the facts are disputed, the sums are significant, or a deadline could matter.
