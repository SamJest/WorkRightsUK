# Batch 33 competition notes

## What this batch focused on
This was a QA and trust batch, not a topic-expansion batch. The target was to remove the kind of placeholder or over-templated wording that makes a small site feel weaker than larger competitors.

## Main gap closed
Competitor pages often look stronger simply because they do not expose scaffolding, old brand names, dummy canonicals or stub copy. Batch 33 removes several of those signals from public pages.

## Next likely priority
Batch 34 should be a post-deploy live QA pass on the rendered site: headings, spacing, top-of-page trust cues, result-state language, and any remaining pages still carrying old scaffold patterns.
