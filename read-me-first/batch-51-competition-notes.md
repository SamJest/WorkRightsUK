# Batch 51 competition notes

## Why this batch
Competitors are often better at the journey after a user realises the package is mixed. WorkRightsUK already has the right pages, but the bridge between them needed to be stronger.

## Gap closed
- Stronger final-pay entry point for last-payslip problems.
- Clearer instruction to split payroll items, redundancy and compensation before comparing totals.
- More obvious routing from notice and holiday checks into the final-pay page.

## Product position
This keeps the site focused. Instead of adding new topics, the update makes one of the most useful existing help pages work harder as a practical decision aid.
