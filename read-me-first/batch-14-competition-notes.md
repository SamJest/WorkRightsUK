# Batch 14 competition notes

## Main live gaps addressed
- Holiday competitors often handled starter/leaver and practical balance scenarios more naturally
- Settlement competitors often made the package feel easier to interpret at a glance and more obviously serious
- Maternity competitors often did a better job of signposting what to do if SMP may not fit

## What Batch 14 changed because of that
- Holiday now includes simple carry-over and bank-holiday context for regular-pattern users
- Settlement now pushes harder on what should be verified before relying on the figure
- Maternity now pushes harder on the next practical route if SMP may not fit
- A new settlement next-step page was added to convert the figure into a more practical decision flow
