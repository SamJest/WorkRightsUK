Last reviewed: 28 March 2026. This page explains what WorkRightsUK is for, what it is not for, and where users should go next if they need specialist help.

What this site is for

Clear practical tools and plain-English support pages around common UK employment-rights questions.

What this site is not for

It is not a law firm, not regulated legal advice, and not a substitute for payroll, tax, union or solicitor advice on complex cases.

When to use this site

Good fit

Quick first-check questions about redundancy pay, notice, statutory holiday, final pay, or settlement-package basics
People trying to understand the main official rule points before deciding what to do next
Workers who want official source links in one place
When to go further

Get specialist help

If you are about to sign a settlement agreement
If you may have a tribunal claim or discrimination issue
If the tax treatment or payroll treatment of a payment package is disputed
