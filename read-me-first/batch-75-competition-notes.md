# Batch 75 — competition notes

## Why this batch
The final-pay page is already one of WorkRightsUK's strongest pages, but strong competitors still win by making the **first action** extremely obvious.

## Competitor pattern worth matching and improving on
- **Acas** is strong at the basic sequence: final pay can differ from normal pay, holiday affects final pay, and notice-pay rules need checking separately.
- **Citizens Advice** style pages often feel easier because they tell users what to check first on the payslip before they branch into other issues.

## Gap closed in this batch
WorkRightsUK already had the right content, but this batch pushes the user harder towards:
1. splitting the package into lines first
2. checking payroll-style lines first
3. only then moving into redundancy, settlement, or dismissal-fairness routes

That makes the page a clearer **bridge** between calculators and leaving-work problems.
