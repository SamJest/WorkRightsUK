What changes these estimates most

Sheet overlap, lap lengths, stock-bar sizes, spacing assumptions, and whether the reinforcement needs extra detailing around openings or edges are the main levers.

Where people under-order

A simple area estimate can miss laps, edge conditions, chairs, tying wire, and the extra lengths needed around corners or penetrations.

Before you order

Check engineer or supplier requirements for bar size, mesh type, laps, spacers, and whether extra reinforcement is needed in specific parts of the slab or footing.
