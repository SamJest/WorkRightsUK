# Batch 71 — Competition notes

## Main gap addressed
The core calculators and leaving-work routes were already stronger than before, but the trust and support pages still sounded more like project notes than finished public pages.

## What stronger competitors do better
- Official and professional competitors keep trust pages short, direct and public-facing.
- They explain review dates and sources without sounding like they are describing their own build process.
- They sound authoritative before the user reaches the calculator, not just after.

## What this batch closes
- Less inward-facing wording on About and Sources.
- Cleaner explanation of checked dates and source links.
- Better match between the product quality of the tools and the tone of the support pages.
