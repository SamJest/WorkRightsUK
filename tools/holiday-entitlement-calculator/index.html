Holiday owed and leaving-work tool

Holiday Entitlement Calculator

What this tool helps you check

Use it for regular working patterns to estimate statutory holiday entitlement, simple starter and leaver positions, and whether there may be holiday left to pay when someone leaves.

This tool is best for straightforward weekly patterns. If the hours or working year are irregular, the official GOV.UK calculator route is safer than relying on a simplified estimate.

Important:

The entitlement figure is not always the same as the money owed in final pay. Carry-over rules, holiday already taken, whether bank holidays are included, and the employer’s holiday policy can all change the practical outcome.
