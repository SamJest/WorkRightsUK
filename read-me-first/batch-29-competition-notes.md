# Batch 29 - Competitor-led UX notes

## Competitor pattern worth matching
The best competitor pages usually feel cleaner and calmer above the fold on mobile. Their headers are lighter, their navigation is easier to scan, and the first screen focuses attention on the tool rather than the site chrome.

## How this batch responds
- Reduces header friction on mobile.
- Makes the brand treatment feel more like a finished product.
- Improves immediate readability without adding new content.

## Rule for future batches
Keep competitor thinking in the notes, not in visible page copy.
