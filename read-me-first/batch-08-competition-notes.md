# Batch 8 competition findings

## Where competitors still felt stronger
- Better settlement pages show the package in plain-English groups immediately.
- Better comparison/explainer pages diagnose the user’s issue faster instead of only defining terms.
- Better redundancy pages explain what to check next, especially contractual terms and whether other money might still be owed.

## What Batch 8 changed because of that
- The settlement checker now produces a clearer at-a-glance breakdown and chooses a likely best next page.
- The comparison page now behaves more like a triage page than a glossary page.
- The redundancy page now makes the “what to check next” step more obvious.
- A basic WebPage structured-data layer was added to rebuilt pages for cleaner search presentation foundations.
