# Batch 56 competition notes

## What competitors still do better
The main remaining weakness after Batch 55 was not feature coverage. It was public tone on the trust and hub pages. Strong competitors and official sites sound settled and user-facing. They do not explain their own build philosophy to visitors.

## Gap closed here
This batch removes the last obvious “builder note” type language from the pages users see when deciding whether to trust the site.

## What changed in competitor terms
- About now reads more like a finished public explanation and less like founder notes.
- Sources now feels more like an authority-check page and less like a meta explanation of why sources exist.
- Tools now leads with the user benefit instead of explaining why the site does not cover everything.

## Why this matters
This kind of trust tone is where solicitor-led competitors often feel stronger even when the actual product is not better.
