About WorkRightsUK

WorkRightsUK helps people check common UK employment-rights questions with practical tools, plain-English guides, and direct links to the official sources behind the core rules.

What WorkRightsUK is for

  • A focused site for the work-rights questions people usually need to answer first
  • Calculator-led and route-led, so it is easier to reach the right first check quickly
  • Built around clear next steps, not just raw figures
  • Checked against official sources on the core pages where rules, rates, dates or caps matter
  • Clear about scope limits, so users know when a page is only a starting point
  • Designed to point users to the most useful next page instead of leaving them with one isolated answer

How we check pages

Clear checked dates and official source links

Core pages show when they were checked, which official pages sit behind them, and when it makes sense to recheck the linked material.

What WorkRightsUK is not for

  • It is not legal advice
  • It is not a law firm site
  • It does not try to cover every employment-law topic or every tribunal scenario
  • It is not a substitute for specialist advice where facts are disputed, deadlines matter, or contract terms are unusual
