What affects estimates most

Dimensions, depth or coverage assumptions, waste allowance, and pack or stock-length rounding are usually the biggest drivers of the final buying number.

Why these guides are useful

The extra guides in each tool set help explain common mistakes, waste allowances, and buying choices that a simple quantity figure cannot cover on its own.
