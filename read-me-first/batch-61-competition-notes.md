# Batch 61 competition notes

## Competitor gap targeted
Solicitor-led settlement tools usually make the package split easier to understand at a glance. Their stronger pattern is not better law; it is clearer framing of which parts are payroll-style pay, which parts may sit in a compensation bucket, and why the headline number can be misleading.

## WorkRightsUK response in this batch
- Surface the likely payroll-style chunk earlier.
- Explain take-home risk without pretending to calculate exact PAYE or legal outcomes.
- Strengthen the written-agreement checks so users know what still needs verifying after the first split.
- Keep the route guidance neutral and practical rather than sales-led.
