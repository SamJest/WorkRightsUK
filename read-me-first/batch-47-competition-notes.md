# Batch 47 competition notes

## Main competitor gap addressed
MoneyHelper is stronger than most redundancy calculators at what happens after the number appears. It gives users tailored help and clearer next steps, while GOV.UK and Acas remain the official baseline for the rule set and caps.

## How Batch 47 closes that gap
- Moves the WorkRightsUK redundancy result closer to a guided decision flow.
- Makes the statutory minimum vs enhanced-package distinction more explicit.
- Helps users spot when a higher leaving-work total is only a mixed package, not a better redundancy figure.
- Adds FAQ coverage to improve both clarity and search presentation.

## Still intentionally avoided
- No broad topic expansion.
- No legal-services style sales copy.
- No attempt to duplicate every edge case handled elsewhere.
