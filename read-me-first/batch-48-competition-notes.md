# Batch 48 competition notes

## Competitor gap targeted
The strongest competitor gap on holiday was not raw page count. It was route clarity. GOV.UK is strong at branching users into the right holiday route, especially where irregular-hours or part-year accrual rules apply. Some commercial competitors also make holiday tools feel more scenario-led even when their legal grounding is weaker.

## What this batch improves
- Stronger pre-calculation route cues for standard, starter/leaver and irregular-hours cases.
- Stronger result interpretation after the user gets a holiday figure.
- Cleaner handoff from holiday into final pay or notice when the real issue is a mixed leaving-work package.

## What still matters next
The next useful improvement after this is likely the settlement/final-pay diagnostic layer or a deeper compare-page triage pass, because those are the places where mixed issue journeys still matter most.
