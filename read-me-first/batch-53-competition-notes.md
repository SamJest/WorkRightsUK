# Batch 53 competition notes

## Competitor-informed aim
The goal in this batch was not to make WorkRightsUK look like a law-firm site. It was to borrow the strongest trust cues used by better competitors — freshness, source clarity and review confidence — while keeping the site plain-English and product-like.

## Competitor strengths worth borrowing
- clearer freshness/review signals near the top of a page
- obvious official-source backing on money and leaving-work pages
- trust cues that tell the user when a figure is only a first check

## Gap closed in this batch
WorkRightsUK already had source links and reviewed dates on many pages, but the presentation was uneven. This batch makes those cues more consistent and easier to understand on the strongest routes.
