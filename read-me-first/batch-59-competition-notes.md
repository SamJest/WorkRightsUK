# Batch 59 competition notes

## Why this batch
The redundancy route was already one of the strongest pages on the site, but the main remaining gap with strong calculator competitors was post-result depth.

## Competitor-informed direction
- the best competitor pattern is not a different formula; it is a clearer sequence after the result
- users need help separating statutory redundancy from the rest of a leaving-work package
- a higher employer total should not be treated as enhanced redundancy until notice, holiday, wages and any extra severance have been separated out

## What this batch closes
- stronger ordered next steps after the redundancy estimate
- clearer package-splitting guidance on the page and inside the result itself
- better route handoff from redundancy into notice, final pay, and compare
