What changes floor prep quantities most

Substrate condition, finished depth, overlap rules, and the difference between nominal product coverage and real site yield are the usual drivers.

Where people under-order

Levelling products and membranes are often estimated from room size alone without allowing for overlap, irregular areas, or extra depth in low spots.

Before you order

Check the intended layer thickness, substrate condition, overlap requirements, and whether primer, tape, edge strip, or moisture control products need to be bought as well.
