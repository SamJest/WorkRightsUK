# Batch 89 competition notes

This batch focuses on rendered usability rather than topic expansion.

## Why this matters
Competitors with weaker legal framing can still feel easier on mobile because:
- anchored sections land in the right place below sticky headers
- FAQ panels open and scroll predictably
- result links stack cleanly on smaller screens
- viewport changes after form submission do not leave the result hidden too high on the page

## WorkRightsUK goal
Make the rendered experience feel calmer and more deliberate on phones, especially on the core result pages, before the Batch 90 checkpoint.
