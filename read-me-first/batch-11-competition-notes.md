# Batch 11 competition notes

## Main parity-to-advantage move
The site is no longer just trying to match competitor calculators. It is now trying to be easier to interpret immediately after the result, which is where a lot of competing pages still rely on generic article copy or commercial lead prompts.

## Remaining notable competitor strengths
- Some holiday competitors still go further on irregular-hours and full holiday-pay-on-leaving calculations
- Some legal competitors still project more expertise because of stronger branded-authority cues
- Some broad consumer-help sites still give richer budget or planning context after redundancy results
