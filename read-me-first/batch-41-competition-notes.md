# Batch 41 competition notes

## Competitor pattern addressed
Competitors in family-rights content often perform better when they do three things well:
1. tell the user which route fits their situation
2. route quickly into the next official step
3. keep guide, calculator and comparison journeys tightly connected

## Gap closed in this batch
WorkRightsUK already had the core family pages, but the journey between them was weaker than the best route-based competitors. Some family links were still relative in a way that could break from nested pages, and the comparison page did not push the next best page strongly enough.

## What Batch 41 improves
- clearer maternity vs paternity vs shared parental routing
- stronger fallback route into Maternity Allowance / next-step help
- better cross-linking between calculators and guides
- calmer, more product-like wording on family pages
