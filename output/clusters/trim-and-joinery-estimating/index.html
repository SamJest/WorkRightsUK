What changes these estimates most

Stock length choices, mitres, waste at corners, and whether the room or opening sizes divide neatly into the available lengths all affect the final order.

When to add more waste

Increase the allowance where there are lots of corners, detailed profiles, damaged walls, or a need for cleaner grain or finish matching.

Before you order

Check profile size, stock lengths, corner waste, and whether adhesive, pins, sealant, or caulk should be planned alongside the trim itself.
