# Batch 84 — Competition notes

## Competitor gap addressed
Solicitor-led settlement tools are usually strongest at the first few seconds after a result appears. They make the user feel the difference between the headline offer and the likely take-home position immediately, then point the user toward the written checks that matter most.

## What this batch closes
- stronger headline-total versus likely take-home framing
- clearer separation of payroll-style lines and compensation-style lines
- sharper instruction to ask for the written breakdown before relying on the headline number
- better cross-routing into final pay and compare when the package is mixed

## Why this matters
This keeps WorkRightsUK practical and source-backed while borrowing the best clarity patterns from settlement competitors without becoming sales-led or law-firm styled.
