# Batch 7 competition findings

## Main live patterns seen in stronger settlement competitors
- They break the package into categories users can understand quickly instead of only showing one figure.
- They use stronger trust framing around review dates and official/legal sources.
- They route users into the right next page or next calculation instead of trapping them on a single calculator page.
- Their strongest pages help users identify whether they are really dealing with redundancy pay, final pay, or a negotiated settlement package.

## What Batch 7 changed because of that
- The settlement checker now gives a clearer package-shape output and stronger next-step prompts.
- A new comparison page was added to route users to the right tool faster.
- Compare was added to site navigation.
- Internal CTAs from the settlement and help sections now push users toward the right next page.
