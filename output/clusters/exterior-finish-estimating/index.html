What changes these estimates most

Overlap, exposed edge detail, surface texture, and the difference between nominal product coverage and real installed coverage can all shift the order.

When to be more cautious

Increase the allowance on weathered or uneven walls, detailed elevations, and jobs where extra finish coats or cut-heavy layouts are likely.

Before you order

Check coverage per pack or board, fixing systems, trims, overlap rules, and whether primers, battens, or membranes need their own quantities.
