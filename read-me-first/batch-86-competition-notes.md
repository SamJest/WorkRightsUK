# Batch 86 competition notes

## Why this batch
The leaving-work compare page is already a strong differentiator for WorkRightsUK, but the remaining gap with stronger competitors is speed of decision after the user lands. The page still had the right content, but it explained too much before making the first route choice feel immediate.

## What competitors do better
- Stronger consumer-help and calculator competitors usually reduce the first decision to one urgent question.
- Good triage flows work from the first document the user has, rather than asking the user to understand the whole package first.
- Simpler route pages feel more confident because they do not encourage the user to open everything at once.

## How Batch 86 closes the gap
- pushes the compare page harder toward one first route
- makes the document-first choice even clearer
- makes final pay the clearest bridge when the immediate problem is a payslip or payroll-style lines
- keeps the family comparison routes separate so the leaving-work compare page stays focused
