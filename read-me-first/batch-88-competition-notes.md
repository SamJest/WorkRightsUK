# Batch 88 competition notes

## Why this batch matters
At this stage the main competitive gaps are no longer broad content gaps. They are search-surface clarity, instrumentation confidence and trust verification.

## Competitor pattern
- GOV.UK and MoneyHelper present extremely clean search snippets and stable metadata
- stronger settlement competitors feel more trustworthy partly because their pages surface review freshness and structured search signals consistently
- technical confidence matters more once the user-facing product is already strong

## What this batch closes
- improves local verification before deploy
- makes it easier to confirm GA4 and JSON-LD after deployment
- standardises robots directives for better snippet and image-preview handling
