# Batch 37 updates

## Focus
Sitewide wording sweep to make the working build sound more human, less process-heavy, and less templated before the next deployment checkpoint.

## Main changes
- softened homepage trust/method wording
- renamed the methodology call-to-action to a more user-facing label
- changed the about-page heading from a process-heavy label to a clearer public-facing one
- replaced repeated "built around official sources" phrasing on tool pages with more natural wording
- kept the cumulative build intact with no GA duplication

## Why this batch
The biggest remaining weakness in the offline build was tone, not scope. Several pages still sounded like they were explaining how the site was made rather than simply helping the user. Batch 37 reduces that without expanding the site.
