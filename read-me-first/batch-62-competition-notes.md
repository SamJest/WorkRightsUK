# Batch 62 competition notes

Competitor pattern addressed in this batch:
- Acas and Citizens Advice are strong at simple final-pay sequencing and last-payslip framing
- solicitor-led settlement pages often tell users to split mixed packages before judging the headline total
- WorkRightsUK can beat both by making final pay the clearest bridge page when payroll items, redundancy and settlement wording overlap

Batch 62 closes that gap by making the final-pay route more decisive and by echoing the same route order into the compare and help pages.
