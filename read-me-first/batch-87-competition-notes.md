# Batch 87 — competition notes

## Why this batch
The unfair-dismissal estimator was already live and useful, but it still lagged stronger guidance-driven competitors in one area: helping users decide from the document in front of them whether unfair dismissal is really the right first route.

## Gap closed
- stronger first-document guidance
- clearer distinction between dismissal-fairness questions and payroll-style questions
- tighter route integration with final pay and compare

## Competitive aim
Make WorkRightsUK's unfair-dismissal route feel more like a finished decision tool and less like a specialist page users only reach after guessing correctly.
