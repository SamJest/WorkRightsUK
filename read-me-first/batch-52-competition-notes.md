# Batch 52 competition notes

## Competitor-informed gap targeted
The main gap was not missing topics. It was that some hub pages still felt like collections rather than curated journeys. Good competitor sites often do a slightly better job of moving users from "I have a question" to "open this page next" without making them scan too much.

## What this batch tries to beat
- Faster route choice from hub pages.
- Better links between comparison pages, calculators, guides, and next-step help.
- More stable public labels on hub cards.

## Practical effect
The help section should now work more like a handoff layer for the rest of the site, especially for final pay, settlement, redundancy, and family-rights journeys.
